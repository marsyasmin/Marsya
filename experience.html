INTERNSHIP EXPERIENCE

Perbadanan Perpustakaan Awam Kelantan

I had the chance to do the internship at Perbadanan Perpustakaan Awam Kelantan (PPAK). This experience helped me gain knowledge in the basic operations of the library and managing information in library. Some of the activities I participated in at the library included record arrangement, assisting people in library daily, learning about cataloging, shelving and more. The internship provided me with application of knowledge I acquired from UiTM and I also get to learn more about the information agency in aiding the community.
